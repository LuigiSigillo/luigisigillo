### 👨‍🔬 About Me

🧪 I am a **Postdoctoral Fellow** at the **[European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL)](https://www.embl.org/)** working with the **[Fabio Petroni](https://www.fabiopetroni.com/) group**.  
We build AI systems that combine **multimodal reasoning**, **agentic exploration**, **tool usage**, **knowledge retrieval**, and **lab-in-the-loop interaction** to accelerate scientific discovery.

🎓 I earned my **Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT)** from **[Sapienza University of Rome](https://www.uniroma1.it/en/)**, where I was part of the **[ISPAMM Lab](https://www.ispamm.it/)** under the supervision of **[Danilo Comminiello](https://danilocomminiello.site.uniroma1.it/)**.

During my PhD I was a visiting researcher at the **[VUG Lab](https://shengfenghe.github.io/group)** at **[SMU School of Computing and Information Systems](https://scis.smu.edu.sg/)** in Singapore, working with **[Shengfeng He](https://shengfenghe.github.io/)**.

Previously, I worked as a **Machine Learning Engineer** at **[Omninext](https://omninext.it/)** and completed my **MSc (Sapienza)** and **BSc (Roma Tre)** in **Computer Science Engineering**.
